About Brickhaus Construction LTD

Brickhaus Construction LTD is a specialist brickwork contractor based in Basingstoke, delivering quality residential brickwork across Hampshire and the Surrounding Areas. From garden walls and extensions to new builds and full restorations — we do the work ourselves, and we do it properly.

We don't subcontract our standards. Every project we take on is managed and delivered by people who take pride in the work — because the work carries our name.
